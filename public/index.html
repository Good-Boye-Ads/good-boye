goodBoye

Interested in a pet? Download goodBoye, a browser extension that turns pesky consumer ads into cute adoption ads from shelters!

Learn more

Shelters, click here to submit adoption info!

How to Use

Installing & Using Extension

Click Install Extension below to view instructions on building the extension for personal use.

Once the extension is installed and enabled on your browser, blocked content and ads will be replaced with images of pets in need of a home.

Click the images to be taken to the shelter's adoption page and consider fostering or donating money if you like what they do.

Contributing an Adoption Ad

If you are a shelter interested in contributing an advertisement, please visit our Ad Submission page.

Fill out our Adoption Ad Form completely and hit Submit Form.

Soon, your adoptive pet will appear as a random advertisement on web pages everywhere. How neat!
